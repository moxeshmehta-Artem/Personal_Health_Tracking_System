Health Tips

  • Eat plenty of fruits and vegetables daily.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking at least 8 glasses of water.
  • Reduce sugar and salt intake.
  • Choose whole grains over processed options.
  • Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate activity daily.
  • Incorporate strength training twice a week.
  • Take short breaks to stretch if you sit for long periods.
  • Find physical activities you enjoy to stay consistent.
  • Get 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night.
  • Practice stress management techniques like meditation or deep breathing.
  • Maintain social connections with friends and family.
  • Limit screen time before bed.
